Spaghetti Carbonara
ingredients: 200g spaghetti,100g pancetta,2 large eggs,50g grated Parmesan cheese,2 cloves garlic,Salt and black pepper,1 tbsp olive oil.
instructions: Boil spaghetti in salted water until al dente.,Heat olive oil in a pan and cook pancetta with crushed garlic until crispy. Discard garlic.,Whisk eggs and Parmesan together in a bowl.,Drain spaghetti and mix quickly with pancetta.,Remove from heat and mix in the egg and cheese mixture.,Season with salt and black pepper. Serve immediately.
